Ok, we're confusing measurements here. Moments are meant for solar days, with hours of varying length throughout the year.

Atoms and Ounces are meant to be subdivisions of moments - so they also are meant for solar days. Sundial stuff, not mechanical clock stuff.

But this is ignoring some of that.

The "AM" and "PM" run at the speed of what would be a 24 hour clock. The upside-down-ness is to further promote confusion.
